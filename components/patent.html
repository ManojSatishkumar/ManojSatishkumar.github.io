Unified Medical Record Cloud Network

The Unified Medical Record (UMR) Cloud Network allows the utilization of FHIR resources of a patient and enables an app store approach specifically for FHIR. This enables the patient to have a web portal with apps containing business logic and provide individual solutions.

Patient Web Portal

Unified Records

FHIR RESTful APIs

Standardized healthcare APIs enable the cloud network and hosted apps to process and deliver targeted solutions.
