UMR Cloud Network

Cerner Healthcare Solutions
#16/235,857

Unified Medical Record (UMR) cloud network is a revolutionary platform that transforms how patients access and manage their complete medical history. By leveraging FHIR standards, this innovation creates a unified healthcare ecosystem where patients own their data and can seamlessly connect with specialized healthcare applications eliminating the burden of maintaining scattered medical records across providers and a lifetime.

Patient Web Portal

Personalized portal with healthcare apps containing business logic for individual solutions.

Unified Records

Eliminates the need to maintain medical documentation, clinical reports, and medication history over a lifetime.

FHIR RESTful APIs

Standardized healthcare APIs enable the cloud network and hosted apps to process and deliver targeted solutions.

Healthcare App Store

App store approach specifically for FHIR, enabling third-party healthcare applications and integrations.
