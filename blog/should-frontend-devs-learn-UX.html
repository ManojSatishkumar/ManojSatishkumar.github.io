Imagination is the root of all happenings around us. Whatever humans have built on this planet started with somebody who imagined it first. It is a privilege to all human beings that we have a vivid sense of imagination.

Now,

Many times, you as a frontend developer will build things and give feedback to UX designers regarding what is available in the market or calculate certain limitations of the system under consideration. The UX designer knows what is best for the business. But you will have to play the role of a balancer who knows the system and estimates the feasibility.

During the process of this feasibility check, you will have to imagine the UX with high-quality UX standards so that the proposal gets accepted by stakeholders and the product becomes successful in the future when it gets released.

So invest time in learning industry UX standards.
