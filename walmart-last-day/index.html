A letter from Manoj Bangalore 22-04-2026

Dear colleagues,

As I write this note on my last working day at Walmart, I find myself pausing more than typing. It is not easy to put years of learning, collaboration, laughter, pressure, ownership, and friendship into a few paragraphs. But if there is one feeling that stands above everything else today, it is gratitude.

Thank you for making Walmart more than just a workplace for me. It became a place where I learned to build with scale in mind, think deeply about customers, respect operational excellence, and appreciate the power of a team that truly shows up for one another. Every design discussion, every production issue, every code review, every release, every brainstorming call, and every small hallway-style conversation added something meaningful to my journey.

I am especially grateful for the trust I received here. Trust to own important problems. Trust to propose ideas. Trust to make decisions. Trust to learn from mistakes. Trust to grow into a better engineer, architect, teammate, and human being. That trust is something I will always carry with pride.

What I will remember most is not only the systems we built, but the spirit with which we built them. The patience during tough debugging sessions. The honesty in architecture conversations. The quick help when someone was blocked. The celebration after a launch. The calmness during incidents. The humor that appeared exactly when the day needed it.

To every colleague I worked with directly: thank you for your partnership, your reviews, your feedback, your energy, and your confidence in me. To those I crossed paths with only briefly: thank you for being part of the environment that made this journey memorable. And to my team: you have my deepest respect. I am proud of what we built, but I am even more proud of how we built it together.

Leaving is emotional, but it is also beautiful in its own way. It means the chapter mattered. It means the people mattered. It means the work left a mark. I may be stepping away from my role, but I am not stepping away from the memories, the lessons, or the relationships that Walmart gave me.

I wish each of you continued success, meaningful growth, bold ideas, kind teammates, smooth releases, and the courage to keep building things that matter. I will always cheer for you and for the incredible work happening here.

Thank you for being part of my Walmart story. It has been an honor, a privilege, and a chapter I will always remember with a full heart.

With gratitude and warm wishes,

Manoj Satishkumar
